    Clean and Care

    Here are some of helpful advice and tips to make your Hers Hijab last longer and drapes beautifully

    Hand wash with only cold or lukewarm water using light detergent

    For stains, spot-treat with a delicate stain remover

    No dry clean.

    Hang the hijab to dry.

    Iron using low heat and avoid applying heat on the Swarovski crystals.

    The Swarovski crystals and pearls can be wiped gently with a jewelry cleaner.
